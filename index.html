📜 Official Tournament Rules

1. Total Teams: 8 Teams
2. Maximum Squad Size: 20 Players
3. Each Team will play 7 League Matches.
4. Top 4 Teams will qualify for the Semi Finals.
5. Winners of both Semi Finals will play the Grand Final.
6. Tournament Start Date: 03/10/2026
7. Age Rule: Maximum 2 Under-30 players, minimum 8 players aged 30+ and minimum 2 players aged 40+.
8. Active KCA A Division players are not allowed. Players who have not played KCA A Division for the last 2 years are eligible.
9. Every player must provide a valid Government ID for age verification.
10. If any team fields a player using a fake ID or incorrect age details, that team will not receive the match points. The player will be banned from the tournament and the Tournament Committee may take further disciplinary action.
11. The decision of the Tournament Committee shall be final and binding on all teams and players.
